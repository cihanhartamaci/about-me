Welcome to the New Setup

For the longest time, I tried to cram everything into one digital identity. My professional work as an EDI Integeration Architect requires a certain level of precision, technical depth, and frankly, a bit of a serious face.

But that's not all of who I am. I have interests in lifestyle, technology trends, and social media that don't always fit into a "Developer Portal" aesthetic.

The Dual Mode Solution

So, I decided to split the difference. Or rather, split the site.

Mode 1: Professional remains my digital CV and technical documentation hub. It's dark, monospaced, and focused on code.

Mode 2: Personal (where you are right now) is for everything else. It's light, readable, and open to broader topics.

Feel free to toggle between them using the button in the navigation bar. I hope this gives you a better picture of the whole person behind the code.
